### Playbook: Geopolitical / Oil-Shock Channel

**Event:** Significant geopolitical event affecting global oil supply or major shipping chokepoint — Strait of Hormuz / Suez / Bab el-Mandeb closure, major-producer war or supply disruption, OPEC+ shock production cut, sanctions on top-5 producer.

**Typical timeline:**
- Acute precursor (kinetic event or chokepoint closure): T+0
- Oil price spike T+0 to T+5 days: typically +10 to +50% Brent depending on magnitude
- BTC reaction T+0 to T+48h: bimodal — initial risk-off DOWN (panic) OR inflation-hedge UP (depending on whether dollar-strength or hard-asset-bid dominates regime)
- Macro feed-through T+1 to T+8 weeks: pipeline inflation (PPI) → consumer inflation (CPI) → CB policy response

**Directional bias:**

| Sub-event | BTC reaction (T+0 to T+48h) | BTC reaction (T+1w to T+4w) | Approx base rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Major chokepoint closure (Hormuz / Suez) | Initial DOWN −2 to −5%, then inflation-hedge bid UP +5 to +15% (if regime supports) | UP, magnitude regime-dependent | ~55% post-initial-shock |
| Major-producer war initiation | Initial DOWN −3 to −8%, partial recovery within week | Mixed, depends on duration / spillover | ~45% (extreme regime-dependence) |
| OPEC+ shock production cut | Small DOWN initial, then UP if inflation-hedge regime active | UP, lower magnitude | ~50% |
| Sanctions on top-5 producer | Small DOWN initial, then drift | Drift continuation | ~50% |

**Regime-dependence is the key variable:**
- If USD-strength regime dominant (Fed hawkish hold/hike): initial DOWN dominates; inflation-hedge bid muted.
- If hard-asset-bid regime dominant (Fed dovish bias OR explicit-inflation-acknowledgement): inflation-hedge bid UP dominates.
- **Structural-regime-overlay META-mechanism (06-05 proposal) applies directly here** — playbook needs overlay-aware sub-cases.

**Trade template (v0 draft):**
- Entry: at T+0 to T+30 min of confirmed event from 2+ primary sources (Bloomberg / Reuters / WSJ / official agency / academic).
- Direction: regime-dependent (see above) — prefer SKIP unless regime read is high-conviction.
- TP: +4% (inflation-hedge bid case) / −3% (risk-off case).
- SL: −2.5% (either direction).
- Time stop: T+48h.
- **Default to SKIP unless regime overlay is high-conviction.**

**Risks:**
- Bimodal reaction (UP vs DOWN) is harder to call than scheduled-macro playbooks — false-confirmation cost.
- Cross-channel transmission (oil → PPI → CPI → CB → BTC) takes weeks; intra-event sub-T+48h move may not capture the dominant directional channel.
- Verification of event from primary sources may lag — require 2+ sources before trading.

**Required state for ENTRY:**
1. Event confirmed from 2+ primary sources (above).
2. Regime overlay read (06-05 META-mechanism) returns high-conviction direction.
3. BTC pre-event volatility within normal envelope (BB width 1h < 4%).
4. No conflicting major event within 48h.
5. Andy approves.
